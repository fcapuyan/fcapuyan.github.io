Website Evaluations Using CRAP Principles

Apple

  • Contrast: Apple uses strong contrast through large typography, whitespace, and high-quality images, making important content easy to identify.
  • Repetition: Fonts, colors, and layout styles are consistent across pages, reinforcing brand identity.
  • Alignment: Content is aligned using a clear grid system, giving the site a clean and professional appearance.
  • Proximity: Related content is grouped logically, making the site easy to scan and understand.

Craigslist

  • Contrast: Craigslist has minimal contrast, with mostly small blue links on a white background, making it difficult to identify important content quickly.
  • Repetition: While link styles repeat, the repetition feels outdated and lacks intentional visual design.
  • Alignment: The page is heavily text-based with limited spacing, causing the layout to feel cluttered.
  • Proximity: Many unrelated links are placed close together, making it hard to distinguish between different categories.
